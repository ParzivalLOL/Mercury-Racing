What is F1 in schools?

F1 in schools is a competition F1 in Schools Ltd is a global not-for-profit organization offering a unique educational experience for students aged 9 to 19. Teams use CAD/CAM software to design, manufacture, and race miniature compressed air cars made from F1 model block. This multi-disciplinary challenge encourages students to apply skills in physics, aerodynamics, design, branding, and finance. Teams raise sponsorships, manage budgets, and compete internationally, promoting Formula 1 to a youth audience. Guided by a founding constitution, F1 in Schools reinvests funds into administering, developing, and expanding the challenge in alignment with Formula 1 guidelines.
